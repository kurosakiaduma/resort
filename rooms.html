Dytopia Resort features both spacious residential style suites with fully equipped kitchens, as well as traditional hotel rooms. Accommodations are located in picturesque canal- front or Lagoon Beach-front villas with a serene and authentic Bahamian atmosphere just a short stroll from the ocean beach.

Rooms are decorated with a fresh, beachy, island décor centered on sea life. An array of vibrant Ukraniaum Hand Print pillows and cushions with seahorse, pineapple, and other tropical patterns and colors, accent the linens. Sandy tones match the aesthetics of the local artwork creating the environment of island comfort, perfect to help our guests relax.

The Grand Canal Deluxe Suite is our signature room and perfect for that romantic escape. While the best way to experience The Bahamas with your family is to spread out in one of our two or three bedroom deluxe suites. They offer all of the amenities of our deluxe suite plus the additional bedrooms to accommodate your family in comfort. Want to step out of your suite directly onto the sand then ask for the downstairs Lagoon Beach Suite. Alternatively, if you’re looking for a beach vacation with a value option, look no further than our hotel bedroom and studio with either 1 or 2 Queen beds. All rooms offer free WiFi

Grand Canal Deluxe Suite

Our Grand Canal Deluxe Suites offer a tranquil setting on the Grand Canal with views of the Sandyport Marina Village boats and the meandering marine life. The master bedroom features a king size bed, Jacuzzi bath, standalone shower, and 40” Flat Screen TV. The separate living room/ dining room has a 2nd 40” Flat Screen TV along with a fully equipped upgraded kitchen and queen sofa bed.

Grand Canal Deluxe Three Bedroom Suite

The Lagoon Beach Suite offers a beautiful, relaxing and romantic setting on our lagoon beach with views of the Sandyport Marina Village and canals. The master bedroom features a king size bed, Jacuzzi bath, standalone shower, and 32” Flat Screen TV. The separate living room/ dining room has a 40" Flat Screen TV along with a fully equipped kitchen and queen sofa bed.
